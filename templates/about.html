ABOUT US

We are AVANII — a purpose-driven company transforming agricultural waste into opportunity

AVANII empowers farmers by purchasing crop stubble and converting it into high-quality biochar using clean, carbon-smart technology. We are committed to reducing air pollution, improving soil health, and building a circular economy that benefits both people and the planet

OUR SERVICES

We provide the perfect service for you.

100% Biochar Production

We convert crop stubble into premium-quality biochar through a sustainable pyrolysis process. Our biochar improves soil health, increases crop yield, and helps in long-term carbon sequestration — making agriculture cleaner and greener

Buying Crop Stubble from Farmers

We directly procure stubble from farmers at fair prices, providing them with a reliable income stream while eliminating the need for stubble burning. AVANII ensures efficient collection, transportation, and eco-friendly use of agricultural residue
